About

PhD geoscientist working at the intersection of machine learning and earth science. I build ML pipelines for subsurface data, drilling tech, and geological systems, including computer vision and LLM-augmented workflows. Background in geophysics (EM, seismic) with prior roles at NSF Unidata, Leidos/NETL, Equinor, and Colorado School of Mines, where I also taught graduate data science courses. Experienced with multi-dimensional data, atmospheric analysis, and the Pangeo stack (Zarr, Xarray). Day-to-day tools include Python (scikit-learn, PyTorch, XGBoost, CatBoost) with experience in deep learning (CNNs, sequence models). Recently getting into Rust. 100+ followers on GitHub.
